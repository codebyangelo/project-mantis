## 7. NIST SP 800-61r2 Alignment & Containment Recommendations
**Incident Lifecycle Phase:** Detection & Analysis -> Transitioning to Containment, Eradication, & Recovery

### Immediate Containment Actions
* Immediately revoke the suspect's network and system credentials.
* Physically seize and isolate the workstation and all associated removable media (USB/CD/DVD).
* Implement a temporary block on all removable storage device mounting via Group Policy Object (GPO) across the affected department.

### Eradication & Recovery
* Perform a full forensic wipe and reimage of the compromised workstation.
* Purge all unauthorized LNK files and temporary artifacts identified in the user's profile.
* Conduct a comprehensive audit of the 'Secret Project' data access logs to identify any additional unauthorized transfers.

### Post-Incident Activity
* Enhance endpoint telemetry to alert on the connection of unauthorized USB devices.
* Integrate stricter DLP policies that prevent the creation of shortcuts to removable media paths.
* Conduct mandatory security awareness training focusing on the risks of data exfiltration and the importance of authorized storage protocols.
